## Metric Explanations

- CTR: Clicks divided by impressions, multiplied by 100.
- CPC: Spend divided by clicks.
- CPL: Spend divided by leads.
- Cost per booked call: Spend divided by booked calls.
- CPA: Spend divided by sales.
- Lead conversion rate: Leads divided by clicks, multiplied by 100.
- Sales conversion rate: Sales divided by leads, multiplied by 100.
- ROAS: Revenue divided by spend.
- Profit estimate: Revenue minus spend.

## ROAS Thresholds

- Strong: 3.0x or higher
- Acceptable: 1.5x to 2.99x
- Weak: below 1.5x

## Wasted Spend Issues

Campaigns may be flagged for:

- Spend but zero sales
- High CPL
- High CPA
- Low ROAS
- Many clicks but few leads
- Leads but no sales

Each finding should include:

- Issue
- Why it matters
- Recommended action
- Priority level

## Recommendation Themes

- Pause weak campaign
- Reduce budget on low ROAS campaign
- Increase budget on strong campaign
- Improve landing page
- Test new creative
- Add negative keywords
- Improve lead form
- Retarget warm leads
- Review sales follow-up
- Split Google Ads and Meta Ads strategy

## 7-Day Optimization Plan

- Day 1: Review spend and ROAS
- Day 2: Pause or reduce weak campaigns
- Day 3: Improve landing page / offer
- Day 4: Test new ads or keywords
- Day 5: Review lead quality
- Day 6: Reallocate budget
- Day 7: Final campaign report and next tests
